## Root Cause Resolution

**Original Problem**
- Single chatClient with 30s timeout for all LLM calls
- Tool-call streams hit deadline mid-body
- Errors: "scanner error: context deadline exceeded"

**Solution**
- Split into two clients: chatClient (2m) and toolsClient (10m)
- Tool-call streams now have 10m timeout
- Chat replies have 2m timeout
- Eliminates deadline exceeded errors for tool execution
